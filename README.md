\# **E-commerce Sales Analytics**



\## Project Overview

Analyzed 10,000+ e-commerce transactions to identify revenue trends,

top-performing products, category performance, and regional sales patterns

using SQL, Python, and Power BI.



\## Business Questions Answered

\- What is the total revenue and profit?

\- Which year/month had highest sales?

\- Which category generates most revenue?

\- Which region performs best?

\- Which products are making a loss?

\- Does discount negatively impact profit?

\- Who are our top customers?



\## Tools Used

\- \*\*Python\*\* → Data Cleaning \& Exploratory Analysis

\- \*\*MySQL\*\* → Business Queries \& Insights

\- \*\*Power BI\*\* → Interactive Dashboard

\- \*\*Excel\*\* → Initial Data Exploration

\## Key Insights

\-  Total Revenue: $2.3M+ across 4 years (2015-2018)

\-  Business showed consistent growth year over year

\-  Technology category generated highest revenue

\-  West region was the top performing region

\-  High discounts (>40%) consistently led to losses

\-  Office Supplies had highest order volume

\-  Consumer segment contributed most to total sales



\## Dataset

\- \*\*Source:\*\* Superstore Sales Dataset

\- \*\*Rows:\*\* 9,994 transactions

\- \*\*Period:\*\* 2015 - 2018

\- \*\*Features:\*\* 21 columns including Sales, Profit, Region, Category
